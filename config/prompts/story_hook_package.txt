## Title rules
- ONE sharp promise the video proves
- Good: "Why Your Brain Sabotages Sleep." / "Entire Procrastination in 15 mins" / "People Who Never Ask for Help"
- BANNED: "The Full Story", "Explained", "Deep Dive", "Shocking", "Unbelievable", generic "Unlock your potential"

## Cold open (First-50 / Hook System)
1. **0-5 sec:** Pattern interrupt — mid-struggle, NOT "psychology is…", NOT "today we explore"
2. **5-15 sec:** Specific proof (study, stat, behavior, quote fragment)
3. **15-45 sec:** Open loop — BUT/THEREFORE ("You think X, BUT Y, THEREFORE Z")
4. **45-60 sec:** Second loop — deeper wrong turn, unanswered

## thumbnail_scene rules
- Edge-to-edge 16:9, cream/beige background, bold black stick-figure line art
- ONE hero subject 40%+ of frame, right-weighted; quieter left third for text overlay later
- Single visual metaphor (brain, mirror, phone, bed, etc.)
- NEVER: letters in image, collage, giant question marks, photorealism, emoji
